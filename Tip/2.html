Mo #3: Trnh by v t ng m - khc ngha
T ng m khc ngha l mt by kh hay gp trong phn 2 ca thi TOEIC.
V d: When will the board meeting begin? Bui hp hi ng khi no bt u?
A. After the president arrives. Sau khi ch tch n.
B. In the conference room. phng hi ngh.
C. We should board the plane now. Chng ta nn ln my bay by gi thi.
cu trn t "board" trong cu hi l mt danh t th hin ngha "hi ng qun tr", cn
t "board" trong p n C li l ng t mang ngha "ln my bay". Nu chng ta ch
nghe long thong thy cu hi c t "board" v nghe cu tr li C cng long thong c
t "board" ri sau kt lun C l p n ng th chng ta c mt c la ngon
mc ri nh.
Mo #4: Trnh by v cc t pht m gn ging nhau nh ngha hon ton khc nhau.
V d: Hasn't the company hired more workers yet? Cng ty thu thm nhn vin
cha?
A. Yes, I'm leaving for work now. Vng, ti ang i lm.
B. Yes, it will get higher. Vng, n s cao hn.
C. They're still interviewing people. H ang cn phng vn.
Nu nghe h c nhanh th "hired" v "higher" s rt d b nhm ln l 1 t vi nhau. V
trong trng hp chng ta nghe khng r c ni dung, kh nng chng ta s b mc
by v t khc m khc ngha ny rt cao.
Mo #5: Trnh by v cu hi v p n cng cp mt ch nhng li khng lin quan
n nhau.
V d: It's going to take a long time to clean the garage. S tn kh lu lau dn ga-
ra y.
A. For about 3 years. Khong 3 nm.
B. Do you need any help? Bn cn gip khng?
C. Yes, it's very clean. Vng, n rt sch.
Nu bn ch nghe c cm "take a long time" th khi nghe p n ch c p n A nhc
n 3 nm v lin quan n thi gian di, v nu chng ta suy ngh theo hng th kh
nng chng ta s dnh by rt cao. Trn thc t p n ng ca cu trn l B.
